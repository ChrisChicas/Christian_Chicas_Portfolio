TechKnows

July 2022

TechKnows was my final project for the software developement bootcamp. I worked in a group on a mock news article website where users could view tech articles and could create an account/log in to write, edit, and delete their own articles/comments on articles. This was the most technically advanced project I have built to date since I had to incorporate all that I learned during the bootcamp while thinking and researching different ways to make all of my ideas come to fruition. Ultimately, TechKnows has user authentication/role based authorization, CRUD functionality, content filtering based on user queries/selections, pagination, and responsive design. This app was built using React, Express, PostgreSQL, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap, and is being hosted on Heroku.
